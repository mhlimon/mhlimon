As per CCR information, I checked historical data and logics of gland steam supply system valves and found out that the valves are working as per control signal, however the fluctuation of pressure arises from somewhere else, the control valve operation is not the cause of the fluctuation, rather it operate in accordance of the pressure, hence it is the effect.
